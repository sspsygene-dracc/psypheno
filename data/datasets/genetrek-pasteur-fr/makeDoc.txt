#!/bin/zsh
# Provenance for the genetrek-pasteur-fr dataset.
#
# Source page:  https://genetrek.pasteur.fr/
#               (Thomas Bourgeron, Institut Pasteur — Human Genetics &
#                Cognitive Function lab. Aggregates ~30 published gene
#                lists relevant to NDD/autism and adds the autism OR
#                from Rolland et al. Nat Med 2023.)
# File:         genetrek-data-2024-04-26.tsv (~19 MB, 61,669 genes)
# Cadence:      irregular release; the snapshot date is in the filename.
#
# The TSV is downloaded **manually** from the GeneTrek site export — the
# site is a Vue SPA with no clean curl-able URL, and the Genome Browser
# track-hub form (jerikoo.free.fr/UCSC/genetrek_pasteur_*.bb) requires
# bigBedToBed which is not available in our local toolchain. Until those
# constraints change, refresh by clicking through the site and saving
# the resulting TSV here under the same filename pattern.
#
# Issue: https://github.com/sspsygene-dracc/psypheno/issues/11
